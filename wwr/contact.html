History

Blue water Rafting company

This company was started by my grandfather after he was kicked out of a similar company. He always wanted to be self-employed, but because of the situation, he got an opportunity to do so. Maisha ni kujaribu, usiogope kuanguka (Life is about trying, don't be afraid of falling). Our mission is to make everyone happy.
